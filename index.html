The Market

Anyone that hears the idea of contactless in-seat delivery for venues knows that it makes sense and it long overdue. Technology has continued to make our world more efficient and fast-paced. The data we compiled from our market research only validated what we were all already believing...It's time for a change.

Our research has validated that:

  • Fans are more likely to order concessions if there was an in-venue mobile ordering app for pick-up or in-seat delivery.
  • If wait times were cut in half, the average fan in the US would spend an average $20.
  • Over half the fans we surveyed are willing to pay more for the convenience of seat vendors accepting card through in-seat delivery.
  • Most of them would also skip the concession line for food and beverages if they could order it from a seat vendor through an app.
